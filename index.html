About

Ruth is a versatile violinist and electric violinist based in Cardiff. She is available for events, tours, weddings, shows and recordings across the UK and around the world. With a vast repertoire of popular and classical music, Ruth can tailor her sets to create the perfect ambience for your event. As a graduate of RWCMD, Ruth has been trained to the highest of standards. Ruth has toured, China, Russia, the EU and the UK extensively. She currently enjoys performing as a soloist and as a member of groups including; ELO AGAIN, the British Paraorchestra, Symphonica, British Sinfonietta and as co-manager of the Fleur Quartet. Recent highlights include recording at Abbey Road for a six-part documentary premiering on Sky Arts in June.
