Weddings & Events

Ruth offers a solo wedding and event package playing a mixture of classical and pop repertoire on either acoustic or electric violin. For weddings, she can perform during the ceremony and two 30 minute sets either before, after or during drinks. For events, she can perform two 45 minute sets. Performances are accompanied by specially made backing tracks. Pricing depends on location, special requests and and timings. Please get in touch for a custom quote.

Recording

Ruth is available for studio and session work on both violin and viola. She also has a home studio for remote recording. Parts can be read from a score, improvised or composed and transcribed by Ruth to compliment your track. Shows & Tours

Ruth is available as an electric or acoustic violinist, violist and backing vocalist for shows and tours. She is a current cast member of ELO AGAIN as electric violinist and backing vocalist and has toured across the UK and internationally with the group. Other recent highlights include performing with So Solid Crew at the O2 and touring to Moscow with the British Paraorchestra.

String Quartet

Ruth manages the Fleur Quartet, a string quartet based in Cardiff. The quartet are available for weddings, event and functions. Please visit www.fleurquartet.com for more information.
